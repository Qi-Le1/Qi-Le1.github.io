Indoor Localization for Car Finding

Indoor positioning provides a new experience for the users, and becomes more and more important for the car-finding in the underground garages. Researchers have come up with many accurate indoor localization systems in the laboratory. However, they can hardly been deployed in the garages due to many challenges in real world. In this work, we propose a smartphone-based indoor positioning system LocateMe, which leverages radio signals and geomagnetic fields to provide fingerprinting-based localization service in garages. In case of the poor network service, we calibrate the dead reckoning by using the Bluetooth Low Energy signals. Our experiments show that the median accuracy of LocateMe can reach 5 meters in both controlled and in-the-wild sce- narios.
